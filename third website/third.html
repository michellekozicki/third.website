Edible Slime

How to Make Edible Slime

You'll Need:

  • 1 can (14 oz.) of sweetened condensed milk

  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch

  • food coloring


instructions


Step 1: Pour a can of sweetened condensed milk into a pan. Add one tablespoon of cornstarch to the mixture and stir.

Step 2: Bring the mixture to a low heat. Continue to stir constantly. If you donâ€™t, the mixture may stick.

Step 3: Remove the mixture from the heat when it has thickened. It should be more gelatinous and harder to stir.

Step 4: Add 10-15 drops of food coloring.

Step 5: Allow the slime to cool, and then you can play with it and eat it!
