Fluffy Slime

How to Make Fluffy Slime

You'll Need:

  • 1/2 cup school glue

  • 6-9 teaspoons of borax

  • 1 cup hot water

  • lotion

  • 1⁄2 cup foaming body wash, or foaming soap

  • more than 1 tablespoon cornstarch

  • 1/2 cup shaving cream

  • food coloring


Instructions


Step 1: Take 1 teaspoon of borax powder and add it into 1 cup of hot water. Mix it until it's thoroughly dissolved and set aside for later use.

Step 2: Pour ½ cup of glue into a separate bowl.

Step 3: Add ½ cup of shaving cream into the same bowl as the glue.

Step 4: Add ½ cup of foaming body wash or soap.

Step 5: Mix until there are no lumps. The substance should have a thick and fluffy texture, similar to marshmallow cream.

Step 6: Add in slightly more than 1 tablespoon of cornstarch and mix thoroughly, but carefully.

Step 7: Add lotion to your slime.

Step 8: Add in food coloring, and mix until there are no white streaks. Continue to stir in 3 teaspoons of the borax mixture to your slime, then continue adding the borax mixture 1-3 teaspoons at a time until the slime is at the consistency you’d like it to be.

Step 9: Knead the slime. if the slime is too sticky, add 1 teaspoon of the borax mixture and carefully knead it in. If your slime is fluffy, but not very stretchy, add a few pumps of lotion into your slime, rub it in, and then knead it again. Repeat as many times necessary!

Step 10: Play with your slime!
