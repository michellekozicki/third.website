How to Make Basic Slime

You'll Need:

  • 1/2 cup of craft glue

  • 1 1/2 cup of warm water

  • food coloring

  • 1 tablespoon borax powder


Instructions


Step 1: In a bowl, mix one tablespoon of borax powder with one cup of warm water. Continue to stir until the borax is completely dissolved.

Step 2: In a different bowl, mix together the glue and Â½ a cup of water. The slime should be a watery, cohesive mixture.

Step 3: Add food coloring to the watery glue mixture. If you want, you can separate the glue mixture evenly into smaller jars and make each one a different color.

Step 4: In a large bowl, mix the borax solution into the watery craft glue solution. Let it sit for 30 seconds to solidify, and then have some fun with your slime!
